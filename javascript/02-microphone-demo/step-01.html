Step 1: Random Numbers Seismograph

Teacher Notes:
This step visualizes random numbers as both a seismograph (line chart) and a jumping circle. The jagged, unpredictable line helps students see the nature of random() over time, while the circle shows the same value as a single point.

Classroom Tips:
- Ask students to describe the pattern they see in the line and the circle.
- Compare this to a real seismograph or heart monitor.
- Discuss how randomness is used in games and simulations.
