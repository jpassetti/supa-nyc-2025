Step 2: Perlin Noise Seismograph

Teacher Notes:
This step visualizes Perlin noise as a seismograph (line chart). The smooth, wave-like line helps students see how noise() produces gradual, organic changes. A circle also moves smoothly for comparison.

Classroom Tips:
- Ask students to compare this to the random seismograph.
- Discuss how smooth transitions are used in animation and design.
- Try changing the noise step size for faster or slower waves.
