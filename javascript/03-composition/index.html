Demo 3: Abstract Compositions with Shapes

  1. Step 1: 12x12 Tile Grid Composition
  2. Step 2: Color Triad Grid Remix
  3. Step 3: Weather Data Grid Visualization

Step 1: Each grid cell randomly draws a black circle, square, or line (no rotation).
Step 2: Each cell randomly draws a colored or white square, and may have a randomly rotated half-circle on top, using a color triad. Some cells are left blank for negative space.
Step 3: Each day of the year is visualized as a circle in a grid, with size and color mapped to Syracuse, NY high temperatures. Blue = cold, red/orange = hot.
